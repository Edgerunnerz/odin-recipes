Hamburger "a la Brent"

Description

A magnate of American cuisine, the hamburger is the perfect 4th of July dish

Ingredients

  • Premium A5 wagyu
  • Potato buns
  • Preferred toppings
  • Cheddar or American cheese

Steps

  1. Season burger patties with signature Brent seasoning and add to grill
  2. Add buns to edge of grill with butter
  3. Add two slices of cheese to patty and assemble burger
  4. Use spicy mayo or sauce of choice to both buns and combine all ingredients
  5. Enjoy your 4th of July with a tasty burger!
